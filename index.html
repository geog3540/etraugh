Geovisualization Portfolio GEOG 3540

Disparities in Adult Education Attainment

This bivariate chloropleth maps 2 education attainment attributes. 1. Percentage of Adults With A Bachelors Degree or Higher 2. Percentage of Adults With A 9th Grade or Under Education. By visualizing these two variables together it highlights areas with a large disparity in education attainment, suggesting the presence of inequities.

Adults With Less Than 9th Grade Education

This is a heat map showing which states have the highest percentage of adults with under a 9th grade education. There's a general trends of lower percentages in the North with Montana, North Dakota, and Michigan. Highest percentages are found in California, New York, and Texas.

Percentage of Adult Population With Diabetes

This map visualizes adult diabates percentages via circle size and sequential coloring. There's a general trend of higher diabetes percentages along the eastern bordern. Further research could explore potential causes behind the East's higher rates.

Education Attainment In The USA

This project compares different levels of education attainment across the United States, compared via a small multiples map.

Amenity Entropy of Iowa City Blocks

This illustrates relative accessability to essential amenities by Iowa City Census blocks. Over 2000 points of interest were filtered into service, health, food, entertainment, and education categories to be compared.
