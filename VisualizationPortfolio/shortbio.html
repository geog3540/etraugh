Ethan Traugh Cartographer

I am a student at the University of Iowa, pursuing a double major in Geographic Information Science and Computer Science. I love communicating and exploring non traditional geographic phenomena via spatial analysis and visualization. Global distribution of music venues, amenity diversity, and human attachment to places are examples of niche topics I have put under the GIS microscope. Beyond my academic pursuits, I have an immense passion for music and audio production. The continuous process of learning to shape sound has cultivated an intensely creative and rigorous approach that I bring to my academics and work. Throughout my journey as a student, I am constantly seeking opportunities to merge my technical skills in computer science and GIS with my love for music and audio production. By blending these passions, I hope to develop innovative solutions in the realm of audio technology and contribute to the ever-evolving landscape of multimedia.
